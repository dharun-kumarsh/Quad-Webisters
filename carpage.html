Mercedes-Benz

  • Founded in 1886, Mercedes-Benz is one of the oldest and most prestigious luxury car brands.
  • Known for their S-Class flagship sedan, which offers outstanding comfort, luxury, and technology.
  • Also produces a range of luxury SUVs and sports cars.

BMW

  • Established in 1916, BMW is a leading German luxury car manufacturer.
  • Offers a wide range of luxury vehicles from the compact 1 Series to the flagship 7 Series sedan and X7 SUV.
  • Known for their sporty driving dynamics and innovative technologies.

Rolls Royce

  • Founded in 1909, Audi is part of the Volkswagen Group and a major player in the luxury car market.
  • Produces the A8 luxury sedan, Q7 and Q8 SUVs, and the high-performance R8 sports car.
  • Focuses on quattro all-wheel drive, advanced infotainment, and sleek, modern design.

Lexus

  • Lexus is the luxury division of Japanese automaker Toyota, established in 1989.
  • Known for their reliable, well-built vehicles with a focus on hybrid technology.
  • The Lexus LS sedan is their flagship model, competing with the Mercedes S-Class and BMW 7 Series.

Jaguar

  • Founded in 1922, Jaguar is a British luxury car brand known for their elegant design and sporty performance.
  • Offers sedans like the XE and XF, as well as the F-PACE SUV and the all-electric I-PACE.
  • Acquired by Tata Motors in 2008, Jaguar is undergoing a resurgence with new models and technologies.

Range Rover

  • Founded in 1948, Range Rover is a British luxury off-road vehicle brand owned by Jaguar Land Rover.
  • Known for their rugged, capable SUVs like the Discovery, Range Rover Sport, and Range Rover Velar.
  • Combines off-road prowess with luxury features and comfort.

Porsche

  • Founded in 1931, Porsche is a German sports car manufacturer known for their high-performance vehicles.
  • Offers models like the iconic 911 sports car, the Cayenne and Macan SUVs, and the all-electric Taycan.
  • Focuses on driving dynamics, precision engineering, and unique design.

Maserati

  • Founded in 1914, Maserati is an Italian luxury and sports car brand known for their style and performance.
  • Offers vehicles like the Quattroporte sedan, Ghibli sports sedan, and Levante SUV.
  • Has a rich history in motorsports and is a symbol of Italian automotive excellence.

Bentley

  • Founded in 1919, Bentley is a British luxury car brand known for their craftsmanship, comfort, and performance.
  • Offers models like the Continental GT coupe, Flying Spur sedan, and Bentayga SUV.
  • Combines classic British style with modern technology and powerful engines.
